------------------------------------------------------------------------------
USAGE                                                               *zoom-usage*

      Use the provided mapping `<C-W>m` on the current split and it will toggle
      it to zoom in and out on subsequent calls.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------
MAPPINGS                                                         *zoom-mappings*

      `<C-W>m`: mapping to toggle zoom of current window
